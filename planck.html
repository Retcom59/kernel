What is Planck?

Planck business model is not to make the seller's job easier, but to provide more convenience to the customer who buys her/his products so that she can sell the product to a wider area.

The main goal of the Planck model is to enable the user to reach the products with less effort. Thus, it aims to increase the market volume of our customers who sell their retail products with the Feynman model.



How does it work?

Planck is basically based on the idea of developing drone ports with elevators, where drones can land and take off.

The planck income model can work fully integrated with the feynman model. In areas where the buildings are tall, the city is crowded and the traffic is heavy, a drone port to be placed on the skyscrapers and an elevator system to deliver the order are developed.





Companies that sell with the Feynman model take the order and bring it to the building. Then it lands on the roof. The drone drops the package into the elevator. The elevator proceeds up to the floor where the order is reached. Thus, the user receives his order without leaving the building, and this operation is completely unmanned.
