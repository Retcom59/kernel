What is Inertia?

Inertia is a business model that brings a new perspective to the grocery delivery industry. It includes 3 different products produced by Kernel Dynamics. Kernel Dynamics aims to prove the concept of delivery of market products by drone with this business model.



How does work?

With the Inertia app, the user selects the products they want to receive, loads them into the basket and confirms the order. Customer inputs order through Inertia App and selects the delivery area

Inertia store is a robot similar to a vending machine. The Inertia store system prepares the customer order and loads it on the Quark V1.0.

Quark flies over to delivery area and lowers customer order to selected area. It makes the delivery with the help of a rope, without going down to human height. This eliminates the security problems that may occur. This eliminates the security problems that may occur.
